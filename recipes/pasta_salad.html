Italian Pasta Salad

Recipe source

Description by ChatGPT

This Italian pasta salad is a delicious and refreshing alternative to a typical sandwich lunch. Made with al dente fusilli, it’s tossed in fragrant pesto, creating a flavorful base that pairs perfectly with fresh ingredients.

The salad is then complemented with juicy cherry tomatoes, creamy mozzarella balls, briny olives, and peppery arugula. It’s a light yet satisfying dish, full of vibrant flavors and textures that make each bite enjoyable.

Ingredients

  • 300 g fusili
  • 250 g cherry tomatoes
  • 285 g mini mozzarella
  • 4 table spoons of green pesto
  • 85 g rucola
  • 90 g green/black olives

Steps

  1. Cook the fusili according to the instructions on the packaging.
  2. Slice the cherry tomatoes, mozzarella, and olives in half.
  3. Drain the paste and rinse with cold water. Let it dry properly.
  4. Mix the fusili with the pesto. After, add everything else and mix
